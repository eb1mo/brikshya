What is Brikshya?

Brikshya is a initiative designed to enhance urban air quality and promote the growth of green spaces. Our platform serves as a comprehensive tool for monitoring air pollution and making informed decisions to combat it. By providing real-time Air Quality Index (AQI) data and identifying dominant pollutants, Brikshysa empowers communities to take actionable steps towards a cleaner and healthier environment. Our interactive map highlights current green spaces and potential areas for new plantings, facilitating strategic urban greening efforts.

WHO WE ARE

We are a passionate team of tech enthusiasts, environmental advocates, and community builders dedicated to creating sustainable urban spaces. Our diverse backgrounds in technology, environmental science, and urban planning converge to bring you Brikshysa, a project that embodies our collective vision for a healthier planet.
